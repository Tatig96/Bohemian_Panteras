Hello, beaches

Beachwalkers United is a unique foundation, because we focus on collecting beach litter or recyclables like plastic and aluminum in order to actively raise funds to help save our beautiful coasts. Our team of anti-pollution activits would rather accept recyclables from your trash as a currency, which means... Put your money where your mouth is and bring us some trash! We feel like this will cause a ripple effect, and not only clean our beaches, but help the cause for a sustainable future.

What do we do with these funds? Well, after we successfully clean up and recyle our findings and donations, we send our cash to help pay our local dive team. These guys take a boat out into ocean and deep dive for industrial fishing gear that pollutes our oceans and hurts our marine life.
